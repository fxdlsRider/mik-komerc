Family-owned. Clean. Tradition since 1990.

For over three decades, mik-komerc has developed and manufactured high-quality chemical cleaning products for professional applications. As a family business, we combine tradition with innovation.

Our Story

Founded in 1990 in Jovanovac, mik-komerc has grown into one of the region's leading manufacturers of chemical cleaning products. What began as a family business is today a trusted partner for companies across Serbia and Europe.

Our products are used in restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and industrial facilities — wherever cleanliness is non-negotiable.

Quality, affordability, and sustainability are not just words to us. They are the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.
